## 📦 Product Fields

### Basic Information

- **Product Name**: Item designation
- **Category**: Type (Ring, Necklace, Bracelet, etc.)
- **Notes**: Additional details

### Physical Properties

- **Gross Weight**: Total weight in grams
- **Quantity**: Number of items
- **Material**: Detected or specified material
- **Purity**: Gold/Silver purity (18K, 925, etc.)

### Commercial Details

- **Purchase Price**: Cost in local currency (₹)
- **Selling Price**: Retail price (optional)
- **Vendor**: Supplier information
- **Storage Location**: Physical location (Shelf A1, Box B2, etc.)

### Status & Tracking

- **Status**: In Stock / Low Stock / Out of Stock
- **Created By**: User who added item
- **Created At**: Timestamp
- **Updated At**: Last modification
- **Deleted At**: Soft delete tracking
